What is a blog for?  You see a ton of blogs being created.  Usually they only have a handful of posts from a few years ago.  How the heck can this blog become anything different?

The reason so many blogs deprecate is: the owners don't really care about them!  At the end of the day, these bloggers are not actually writing for themselves.  They're writing to some perceived high-value audience and putting on a persona in order to write TO them.  

Turns out, that sort of pandering is inauthentic, so it's boring.  And unsustainable on the long run.  So guess what?  This is going to be the exact opposite.  I'm going to write randomly, and only about things that interest me.  There will be no minimum length post, and no general topic.  

Buckle up!
